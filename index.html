Jianmin Wang(王建民)


About

I'm a Ph.D student in department of Integrative Biotechnology & Translational Medicine at Yonsei University in Korean.

My research involves drug discovery, deep learning, chemoinformatics, and bioinformatics. I focus on applying deep learning methods and computational techniques to unlock drug R&D and health computing.
